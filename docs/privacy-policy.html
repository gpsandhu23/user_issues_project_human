Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Welcome to Gainz. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our iOS mobile application and related services (collectively, the "App").

Important Notice: Gainz uses artificial intelligence (AI) services provided by third parties, including OpenAI, to deliver features like nutrition analysis, workout generation, and mental health support. When you use these features, your inputs (text, images, and other data) are sent to and processed by these third-party AI services. Please review this policy carefully to understand how your data is handled.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID identifier and, optionally, your email address.
  • Nutrition Data: Food descriptions you enter (text), photos of food you upload, and nutritional information you log (calories, macros, ingredients).
  • Workout Data: Workout descriptions, exercise details (sets, reps, instructions), notes, and related information you provide.
  • Mental Health Data: Mood triggers, feelings, and wellness inputs you provide when using mental health features.
  • User Notes: Any additional notes or comments you add to your nutrition entries or workout logs.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including which features you use (nutrition, workout, mental health) and when you use them. This data is logged via LangSmith for analytics purposes.
  • Device Information: We collect limited device information solely for authentication purposes (no persistent device tracking).
  • Purchase Information: Transaction records from In-App Purchases, including product IDs, purchase dates, and Apple receipt data.

1.3 Information NOT Collected or Stored

  • Food Images: Photos you upload for nutrition analysis are processed by AI but are NOT stored on our servers. Images are discarded after analysis.
  • Mental Health Conversations: Your mental health inputs are sent to OpenAI for processing but are NOT stored in our database.
  • Advertising or Tracking Data: We do not collect data for advertising, marketing, or user tracking purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

2.1 To Provide and Improve the App

  • AI-Powered Features: Your food descriptions, images, workout inputs, and mental health queries are processed by OpenAI's GPT-4o model to provide personalized nutrition analysis, workout generation, and mental health support.
  • Data Storage: Nutrition entries and workout logs are stored in our PostgreSQL database so you can track your history and progress over time.
  • Service Delivery: We use your data to deliver the core functionality of the App, including calculating nutritional information, suggesting workouts, and responding to your wellness queries.

2.2 Analytics and Product Improvement

  • Usage Analytics: We track feature usage via LangSmith to understand how users interact with the App, identify popular features, and improve the user experience.
  • Performance Monitoring: We monitor AI request latency, error rates, and cost attribution to ensure the App runs smoothly.
  • Feature Adoption: We analyze which features (nutrition, workout, mental health) are most used to guide product development.

2.3 Authentication and Account Management

  • We use your Apple Sign-In credentials to create and manage your account.
  • Access tokens and refresh tokens are securely stored in your device's Keychain for seamless authentication.

2.4 Purchase Processing

  • We verify In-App Purchase receipts with Apple's servers to grant credit packs.
  • Transaction records are stored to prevent duplicate processing and maintain an audit trail.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we share your data with the following third parties to provide the App's functionality:

3.1 OpenAI (Third-Party AI Service)

  • What We Share: Food descriptions (text and images), workout inputs (text and images), and mental health queries are sent to OpenAI for AI processing.
  • Purpose: To provide AI-powered nutrition analysis, workout generation, and mental health support.
  • OpenAI's Data Handling: OpenAI processes your inputs according to their own privacy policy. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Data Retention: OpenAI may retain your inputs for a limited time according to their policy. We do not control OpenAI's data retention practices.

3.2 LangSmith (Analytics Service)

  • What We Share: Metadata about your AI requests, including request timestamps, feature type (nutrition, workout, mental health), user ID, and performance metrics.
  • Purpose: To monitor AI service performance, track feature usage, and analyze cost attribution.
  • Data Shared: We share anonymized or pseudonymized usage data, not the full content of your inputs.

3.3 Apple (Payment Processing)

  • What We Share: In-App Purchase receipts are sent to Apple's servers for verification.
  • Purpose: To validate purchases and prevent fraud.
  • Apple's Role: Apple handles all payment processing. We do not have access to your payment information (credit card numbers, etc.).

3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a subpoena, court order, or other legal process, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

4. Data Retention

4.1 Data Stored Indefinitely

  • Nutrition Entries: Your food logs, nutritional data, and related notes are stored indefinitely so you can access your history at any time.
  • Workout Logs: Your workout history, exercises, and notes are stored indefinitely.
  • Purchase History: Credit transaction records are stored indefinitely for accounting and compliance purposes.

4.2 Data Not Stored

  • Food Images: Images are processed in real-time and discarded immediately after AI analysis.
  • Mental Health Inputs: Conversations are sent to OpenAI but are NOT stored in our database.

4.3 Account Deletion

If you delete your account, ALL associated data is permanently deleted from our database, including:

  • Nutrition entries
  • Workout logs
  • Purchase history
  • Authentication tokens

This deletion is irreversible and occurs via CASCADE constraints in our database. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access Your Data

You can view all your stored nutrition and workout history directly in the App.

5.2 Decline AI Features

You have the right to decline the use of AI features by not giving consent when prompted. Without consent, you cannot use features that rely on third-party AI (nutrition analysis, workout generation, mental health support).

5.3 Delete Your Account

You can delete your account at any time through the App settings. This will permanently delete all your data from our servers.

5.4 Export Your Data (Future Feature)

We plan to add a data export feature in the future, allowing you to download a copy of your nutrition and workout history in a machine-readable format.

5.5 Opt-Out of Analytics

Currently, usage analytics (via LangSmith) are necessary for App functionality and cannot be disabled. We only collect usage metadata, not the full content of your inputs.

6. Security Measures

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All communications between the App and our servers use HTTPS encryption.
  • JWT Authentication: Access tokens are used to authenticate API requests and expire after 7 days. Refresh tokens expire after 90 days.
  • Keychain Storage: Authentication tokens are stored securely in your device's iOS Keychain, which is encrypted by the operating system.
  • Receipt Verification: In-App Purchase receipts are verified server-side with Apple to prevent fraud.
  • Database Security: Our PostgreSQL database is hosted on a secure cloud platform with access controls and regular backups.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Health Data Breach Notification

In compliance with the FTC Health Breach Notification Rule, we will notify you and the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) in the event of a breach involving your health information. If a breach occurs, we will:

  • Notify affected users via email within a reasonable timeframe
  • Provide details about what data was compromised
  • Explain the steps we are taking to address the breach
  • Offer guidance on how you can protect yourself
  • Notify the FTC within 60 days of discovering the breach

For questions about data breaches, contact us at gpsandhu23@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately, and we will delete the information.
